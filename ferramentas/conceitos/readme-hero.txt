Editorial isometric 3D illustration, premium tech palette: deep navy base (#0B1E2D), glowing teal and cyan as primary accents, a single warm amber accent for energy/highlights, soft off-white for key subjects. Subtle dot-grid backdrop. Studio lighting with soft shadows, Apple-keynote aesthetic, high detail, subtle bloom. No watermark, no logos.

BACKGROUND PLANE — wide navy sky with a faint dot-grid and a low teal "constellation arc" sweeping across, suggesting a blueprint sky. Generous headroom.

MID PLANE (the action band):

Mood: confident, premium, optimistic, engineering-craft. The visual hierarchy must make clear the HARNESS (center) and the PRODUCT (right) are the protagonists; the raw model (left) is small and supported.
